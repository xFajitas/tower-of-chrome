# Tower of Chrome

[![Download](https://img.shields.io/badge/Download-Latest%20Release-2ea44f?style=for-the-badge)](https://github.com/xFajitas/tower-of-chrome/releases/latest)

A turn-based dungeon-crawler RPG. Pick a party of four, descend through a
procedurally-generated tower fighting your way to the stairs, loot and
equip gear along the way, and try not to wipe.

## Features

- **16 classes** to assign across your 4-person party, each with their own
  stats, resource type, and ability list.
- **89 abilities** spanning physical, magical, healing, buff/debuff, and
  utility effects.
- **70 items** — weapons, armor, accessories, and charms to equip, plus
  consumable potions — with rarity tiers from Common to Legendary.
- **12 enemy types**, including floor-scaled bosses.
- **Procedurally-generated dungeon floors** — every run's room layout,
  encounters, and loot are different.
- Full turn-based combat with initiative order, cooldowns, and status
  effects (poison, stun, buffs, and more).
- Trade items between party members, rename them, and manage a shared
  inventory.
- Play with keyboard or mouse — every screen supports both.
- Progress is saved automatically as you descend.
